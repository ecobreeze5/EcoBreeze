Bathroom ventilation

The STEM bathroom ventilation system combines humidity, temperature, and air quality sensors with efficient fans, replaceable filters, and mobile app control for optimal air management. It automatically extracts stale air, filters pollutants, and improves comfort while conserving energy, thus extending the life of bathroom structures with its innovative technology.

Types of services

Energy efficiency

An efficient ventilation system consumes little energy while keeping the bathroom ventilated.

Humidity control

It regulate humidity levels in the bathroom to prevent the formation of mold and mildew, thus maintaining a healthier environment.

Low maintenance

It requires little maintenance thanks to features like durable motors and easy-to-clean designs.

Odor elimination

It helps eliminate unwanted odors from the bathroom, especially useful in spaces without natural ventilation.
